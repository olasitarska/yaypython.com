Yay Python! is a beautifully crafted book that will guide you through the wonderful journey of building your first internet website.

Learn & discover all the beautiful details about computers, technology and internet in a creative & imaginative way.

Build your first, complete and amazing website powered by the beauty of Python and Django.

Connect the dots to see how your internet browser goes from request to response. Discover what happens, when you type google.com, and hit enter.

Learn about open source community, and meet the people who are behind it all.

Beautifully crafted, unique and thoughtful because the tiny little details matter.

Easy to understand for everyone who is curious about the way the world around us works. Especially for those with zero prior experience in programming.

A year ago, we wrote and published the Django Girls Tutorial. It's a thoughtful guide on building your own website for people who never wrote a single line of code in their lives. In just a year, this tutorial has been read by almost 100 000 people.

We had an incredible opportunity to see how more than 1 600 women learned to code at our workshops, where 500 mentors from all over the world used our tutorial to help them build a working website in just one day.
